# Business Insights & Recommendations

## Overview
This analysis explores revenue performance, customer contribution, and time-based trends using transactional order data.

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Concentration by Product Category
Revenue is unevenly distributed across product categories, with a small number of categories contributing a disproportionately large share of total revenue.

### 2. Customer Revenue Contribution
A subset of customers contributes significantly more revenue than the average customer, indicating the presence of high-value customers.

### 3. Revenue Trends Over Time
Monthly revenue trends suggest variability over time, which may reflect seasonality, promotions, or changes in customer demand.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize high-performing product categories when allocating marketing spend and inventory.
2. Develop retention strategies focused on high-value customers to protect revenue.
3. Monitor monthly revenue trends to better plan promotions, launches, and supply planning.
